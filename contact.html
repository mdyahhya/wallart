Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to some of our most common inquiries.

Do you support custom sizes or designs?

Yes, absolutely! We specialize in custom-sized metal wall arts, clocks, and mirrors. You can choose a custom size, color finish, or even share a custom reference design with us on WhatsApp or Email, and we will craft it for you.

Is shipping free across India?

Yes! We offer complimentary express delivery across India. All shipments are packed securely in robust plywood crates or high-density cartons and are fully transit-insured for your peace of mind.

What materials and finishes are used?

We use premium, high-grade rust-resistant iron and metal alloys for all our arts, clocks, and mirrors. Each piece is finished with high-quality anti-rust powder coatings and rich metallic paint layers to ensure a flawless lifespan of 20+ years.

How do I hang/install the products?

Hanging our art is very straightforward! All Walltone products feature pre-installed, hidden hanging loops or spacers on the back. This makes them extremely easy to mount on standard wall screws or nails while creating a modern, floating 3D shadow effect.
